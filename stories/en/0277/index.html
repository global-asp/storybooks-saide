Friends Vriende Friends

Zimbili Dlamini, Hlengiwe Zondi

Catherine Groenewald

Darshan Soni

English

Level 1

My name is Simo.

My naam is Simo.

My name is Simo.

I have four friends.

Ek het vier vriende.

I have four friends.

Their names are Zizo, Lele, Sisa and Ayanda.

Hulle name is Zizo, Lele, Sisa en Ayanda.

Their names are Zizo, Lele, Sisa and Ayanda.

My friend Zizo likes to play soccer.

My vriendin Zizo hou daarvan om sokker te speel.

My friend Zizo likes to play soccer.

My friend Lele likes to swim.

My vriendin Lele hou daarvan om te swem.

My friend Lele likes to swim.

My friend Sisa likes to play hide-and-seek.

My vriend Sisa hou daarvan om wegkruipertjie te speel.

My friend Sisa likes to play hide-and-seek.

My friend Ayanda likes to read.

My vriendin Ayanda hou daarvan om te lees.

My friend Ayanda likes to read.

Me? I like to do all the things they like to do.

Ek? Ek hou daarvan om al die dinge te doen wat my vriende doen.

Me? I like to do all the things they like to do.

I play soccer with Zizo.

Ek speel sokker saam met Zizo.

I play soccer with Zizo.

I swim with Lele.

Ek swem saam met Lele.

I swim with Lele.

I play hide-and-seek with Sisa.

Ek speel wegkruipertjie saam met Sisa.

I play hide-and-seek with Sisa.

Come, friend, what do you like?

Kom vriend, waarvan hou jy?

Come, friend, what do you like?

Come, play soccer with us.

Kom speel sokker saam met ons.

Come, play soccer with us.

Come, swim with us.

Kom swem saam met ons.

Come, swim with us.

Come, play hide-andseek with us.

Kom speel wegkruipertjie saam met ons.

Come, play hide-andseek with us.

Come, read with us!

Kom lees saam met ons.

Come, read with us!

Written by: Zimbili Dlamini, Hlengiwe Zondi
Illustrated by: Catherine Groenewald
Read by: Darshan Soni
Language: English
Level 1
