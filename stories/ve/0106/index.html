Lukanyakanya Lwa Namukuru Namu Se Fiets Namukuru's bicycle

Munanga ASb teachers

Wiehan de Jager

Yvonne Mulovhedzi

Venda

Level 2

Namukuru na murathu wawe vho di lugisela u ya tshikoloni.

Namu en haar broer is gereed vir skool.

Namukuru and her brother are ready for school.

Namukuru u namedza murathu kha lukanyakanya.

Namu en haar broer ry saam op haar fiets.

Namukuru carries her brother on her bicycle.

Khonani dzawe dzi a kunguwedza hezwi vha tshi dzhena tshikoloni.

Hulle vriende juig toe hulle by die skool inry.

Their friends cheer for them as they ride into school.

Musi vhe kilasirumuni, pfene la swika nnda. Pfene la namela lukanyakanya la tuwa nalwo!

Terwyl hulle in die klas is, kom ’n apie by die skoolhek in.

While they are in the classroom, a monkey arrives outside. The monkey rides away on Namukuru's bicycle!

Vhana vha pandamedza pfene.

Die kinders jaag die apie.

The children chase the monkey.

Pfene la litsha lukanyakanya fhasi ha muri.

Die apie spring af en los die fiets onder ’n boom.

The monkey leaves the bicycle under a tree.

Pfene la bambela kha tshitangadzime vhukati ha danzhe. La namela ntha ntha ha muri.

Die apie swem na ’n eiland in die middel van die meer. Hy klim hoog in 'n boom in.

The monkey swims to an island in the middle of the lake. He climbs high into a tree.

Pfene la salisa nga tshanda! Vhana vha salisa na vhone. Namukuru a wana lukanyakanya lwawe murahu.

Die apie waai totsiens!

The monkey waves goodbye! The children wave back. Namukuru has her bicycle again.

Written by: Munanga ASb teachers
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Yvonne Mulovhedzi
