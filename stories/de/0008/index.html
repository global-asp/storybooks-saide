Was tust du? Wat doen jy? What are you doing?

Nina Orange

Wiehan de Jager

Espen Stranger-Johannessen

Jula Eberth

German

Level 1

Ich singe.

Ek sing.

I am singing.

Sie winkt.

Sy waai.

She is waving.

Ich klatsche.

Ek klap hande.

I am clapping.

Sie streckt sich.

Sy strek.

She is stretching.

Er ruft.

Hy roep.

He is calling.

Ich beantworte.

Ek antwoord.

I am answering.

Sie hört zu.

Sy luister.

She is listening.

Was tust du?

Wat doen jy?

What are you doing?

Written by: Nina Orange
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Espen Stranger-Johannessen
Read by: Jula Eberth
Language: German
Level 1
