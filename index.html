Storybooks South Africa

Storybooks South Africa is a free open educational resource that promotes literacy and language learning in homes, schools, and communities. It makes 40 stories from the African Storybook available with text and audio in isiZulu, Xhosa, Afrikaans, English, Sepedi, Setswana, Sesotho, Xitsonga, siSwati, Venda, Southern Ndebele, and the most widely spoken other languages of South Africa. Share and enjoy!
